Monitor, Analyze, and Secure Your Network in Real Time

At SkyNet Monitoring, we offer powerful tools to give you full visibility into your network traffic. Our real-time capture and anomaly detection system ensures you can spot issues, prevent attacks, and maintain network health without delays.

  • Capture every packet with Wireshark’s powerful tshark engine.
  • Detect suspicious activity and unusual traffic patterns instantly.

Our system processes thousands of packets per second, providing you with detailed insights, historical logs, and actionable alerts — all through an intuitive dashboard.

Built using industry-standard technologies like FastAPI for lightning-fast backend processing and React for dynamic front-end visualizations, SkyNet Monitoring is designed for businesses, developers, and IT enthusiasts who need reliable network traffic intelligence. We focus on simplicity, performance, and security — giving you the peace of mind that your network is always under your control.
